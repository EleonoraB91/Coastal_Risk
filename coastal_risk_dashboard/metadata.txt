about=Plugin for the assessment and visualisation of coastal erosion risk in the minor Italian islands. Load a coastline vector layer, calculate the CVI from four geomorphological and environmental parameters (geomorphology, slope, land use, exposure), classify coastal segments into 5 risk classes, and export results as high-resolution maps (PNG), data tables (CSV), and summary reports (TXT). Includes an auto-zoom database for 28 Italian minor islands across 8 archipelagos. Requires QGIS 3.16 or later. No external dependencies.
